Background

Hey! My name's Fahad Anwar, and I'm from Sacramento, California. My parents are both immigrants from Pakistan, making me a first-generation college student. I attended Inderkum High School, where I led clubs like the South East Asian Student Association (SEASA) and Friday Night Live (FNL), a club dedicated to bringing awareness to underage substance use. Along with this, I played volleyball and wrestled, all while participating in the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program.

On the academic side, I'm interested in embedded systems, machine learning, robotics, and brain-computer interfaces — the overlap between electronics and the human body is what I find most interesting. Outside of that, I enjoy reading, watching films, following the NFL and UFC, and playing video games.
